Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace is an extremely breath-taking example of the Indo-Sarcenic style of architecture, and is till date one of the biggest palaces in the country.

Brindavan Gardens

Built across the Kaveri river, these beautiful gardens were spread over 60 acres of land and were modeled after the Shalimar gardens of Kashmir.

Somanathapura Temple

The commander of the Hoysala army who founded this place. Also, the Somnathpur temple is a classic example of the stone carvings in Hoysala architecture.

Chamundeshwari Temple

The Chamundeshwari Temple is a traditional Hindu temple located on the eastern edge of Mysore at the height of 1000ft on the Chamundi hills.
